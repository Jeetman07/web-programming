Adventure and safety tips for climbing Mount everest

  • Always hire certified guides and Sherpas for safety.
  • Acclimatize properly to prevent altitude sickness.
  • Carry warm clothing, climbing gear, and emergency supplies.
  • Respect the environment and follow established trekking rules.
